About The African Geomatics Group

Precision. Insight. Impact.

The African Geomatics Group is a premier geospatial consulting firm headquartered in Kenya, specializing in advanced spatial analysis, remote sensing, and GIS solutions tailored for African development challenges.

We bridge cutting-edge technology with local knowledge to deliver actionable insights that drive real-world impact. From land administration to climate resilience, we help organizations make better decisions through geospatial intelligence.

Our Services

End-to-end geospatial solutions from data acquisition to decision-ready insights

Remote Sensing & Earth Observation

High-resolution satellite analysis, cadastral survey, change detection, and time-series monitoring using Sentinel, Landsat, and commercial imagery.

Land Administration & Cadastre

Digital land registration, boundary delineation, and tenure security solutions for governments and communities.

Urban Planning & Smart Cities

Spatial modeling, infrastructure planning, and sustainable urban development strategies for resilient cities.

Environmental & Climate Solutions

Deforestation tracking, carbon mapping, flood risk modeling, and climate adaptation planning for environmental stewardship.

Custom GIS Development

Tailored web GIS platforms, automation scripts, and enterprise spatial systems built to your exact requirements.

Training & Capacity Building

Corporate and institutional training in QGIS, Google Earth Engine, ArcGIS Pro, and GIS programming.
